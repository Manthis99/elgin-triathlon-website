FAQ

The practical stuff.

Do I need a triathlon bike?

No. A safe working bike and helmet are the important pieces.

Is this beginner friendly?

Yes. The Sprint distance is being shaped specifically for first-time triathletes.

Where is the swim?

The venue is still TBD. The page will update once permits and safety plans are confirmed.

Why only $20?

The goal is a simple community race, not a premium race weekend. We will only promise what the budget can support.
